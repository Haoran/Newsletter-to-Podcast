The AI boom hinges on the idea that exponential growth will shape the future. The industry is betting that ever-steepening curves will not only enhance AI technology but also boost human demand and society's capacity to meet AI's needs for chips, power, and data. This belief is reflected in the massive global investments, with trillions of dollars flowing into the sector. However, there's a concern that AI's growth might hit a plateau, which could lead to financial instability. The big question remains: how far can we push the AI curve before it hits its limit?
